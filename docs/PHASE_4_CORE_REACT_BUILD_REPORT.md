## Safety And Claim Constraints Followed

- NOVA is presented as validation-first and safety-first.
- NOVA is described as the current validated platform/proof point.
- M.E.D.I.S. is presented as a future R&D-stage application built on NOVA.
- The page does not claim clinical readiness.
- The page does not claim regulatory approval.
- The page does not claim medicine synthesis is already working.
- The page does not claim patient deployment.
- The page does not claim an approved medical device.
- The page does not claim actuator control is enabled.
- No fake medical, hospital, doctor, patient, or device imagery was added.
