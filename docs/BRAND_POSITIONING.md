# Brand Positioning

## Company

Vesperus

## Tagline

Engineering the future of autonomous healthcare.

## Positioning Statement

Vesperus builds intelligent orchestration, validation, and automation platforms for next-generation medical systems.

## Product Hierarchy

Vesperus = company/brand

NOVA = current validated hardware/software orchestration platform

M.E.D.I.S. = long-term healthcare application built on NOVA

## Brand Tone

- Premium
- Technical
- Credible
- Safety-first
- Deep-tech
- Medical automation
- Investor-ready

## What Not To Claim

- Do not claim medicine synthesis is already working.
- Do not claim clinical readiness.
- Do not claim regulatory approval.
- Do not claim actuator control is enabled.
- Do not claim patient deployment.
