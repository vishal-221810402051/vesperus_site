## Messaging Rules

- Vesperus is the company and brand.
- NOVA is the current validated hardware/software orchestration platform.
- M.E.D.I.S. is the long-term healthcare application built on NOVA.
- The tone should remain premium, technical, credible, safety-first, deep-tech, medical automation focused, and investor-ready.
- NOVA may be described as a current MVP and proof point.
- M.E.D.I.S. must be described as long-term R&D.
- Medical, clinical, regulatory, patient, and actuator-control claims must remain conservative and accurate.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim medicine synthesis is already working.
- Do not claim clinical readiness.
- Do not claim regulatory approval.
- Do not claim actuator control is enabled.
- Do not claim patient deployment.
